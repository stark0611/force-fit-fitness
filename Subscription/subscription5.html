Exercise


Running

1. Start with a walking program
2. Ease into a walk-run program
3. Shorten your stride
4. Give your body a chance to adapt and recover
5. Develop a rhythm, then layer in the intensity

Pilates Ball Exercise

1. Bounce on the ball lightly.
2. Use the ball to perform a sit up.
3. Do a ball lift, do a ball lift.
4. Do a bird dog, perform a back extension.
5. Perform ab balances.

Resistance Band Exercise

1. Stand tall with feet hip width apart. Place the band around your wrists. Retract your shoulders by pulling them back and down to maintain good posture throughout the movement.
2. Raise your arms shoulder width apart and pulse the arms out to the sides.

Cycling

1. Protect your head.
2. Don't pedal in high gear for extended periods of time.
3. Use your gears,Get a proper bike fit.
4. Get the right saddle,Change position while riding.

Tai Chi

1. Take it easy, but don't forget about your posture.
2. Keep your center of gravity low.
3. Focus on fluid, full-body movements.
4. Move with your qi in mind.
5. Be mindful.

Jogging

1. Build up to jogging with regular walks.
2. Warm up for 5-10 minutes before you jog.
3. Alternate between jogging and walking when you start.
4. Practice proper form while jogging.
5. Breathe regularly as you jog.

Squat

1. Stand straight with feet hip-width apart.
2. Tighten your stomach muscles.
3. Lower down, as if sitting in an invisible chair.
4. Straighten your legs to lift back up.
5. Repeat the movement.
